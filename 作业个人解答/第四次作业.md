## 第一题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/1.png)

(1)

由于 $X,Y$ 相互独立

则 

$$\begin{align*}
P(X+Y=n)
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} P(X=k)P(Y=n-k) \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} p(1-p)^{k-1} C_{n-k-1}^{r-1}p^r(1-p)^{n-k-r} \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} p^{r+1}(1-p)^{n-r-1}C_{n-k-1}^{r-1} \\
& = p^{r+1}(1-p)^{n-r-1}\sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} C_{n-k-1}^{r-1} \\
& = p^{r+1}(1-p)^{n-r-1}C_{n-1}^{r} \\
\end{align*}$$

即有 $X+Y \sim NB(r+1,p)$

(2)

采用数学归纳法

当 $r=2$ 时，由(1)可得 $X_1+X_2 \sim NB(2,p)$

设当 $r=k$ 时成立，由(1) 得 $X_1+X_2+\ldots +X_{k+1}=(X_1+X_2+\ldots +X_k) + X_{k+1}$

由于 $X_1+X_2+\ldots +X_k \sim NB(k,p),X_{k+1} \sim NB(1,p)$

则有 $X_1+X_2+\ldots +X_{k+1} \sim NB(k+1,p)$ 得证！

## 第二题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/2.png)

(1) 由已知条件 $P(X=x_i \cap Y=y_j | A)=P(X=x_i | A)P(Y=y_j | A)$

有

$$\begin{align*}
E(XY|A)
& = \sum\limits_{i}\sum\limits_{j}x_iy_j \cdot P(X=x_i \cap Y=y_j | A) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i}\sum\limits_{j}x_iy_j \cdot P(X=x_i | A)P(Y=y_j | A) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i} x_iP(X=x_i|A) \sum\limits_{j} y_jP(Y=y_j | A) \\
& = E(X|A)E(Y|A) \\
\end{align*}$$

(2) 先证明： $X_i,X_j$ 在给定 $E_{ij}$ 条件下条件独立

有 

$$\begin{align*}
& P(X_i=x_i|E_{ij})=\frac{P(X_i=x_i \cap E_{ij})}{P(E_{ij})}=C_{n-2}^{x_i-1} \cdot (\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
& P(X_j=x_j|E_{ij})=\frac{P(X_j=x_j \cap E_{ij})}{P(E_{ij})}=C_{n-2}^{x_j-1} \cdot (\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
\end{align*}$$

同时有

$$\begin{align*}
P(X_i=x_i \cap X_j=x_j | E_{ij})
& = \frac{P(X_i=x_i \cap X_j=x_j \cap E_{ij})}{P(E_{ij})} \\
& = \frac{C_{n-2}^{x_i-1}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \cdot C_{n-2}^{x_j-1}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \cdot \frac{1}{2}}{\frac{1}{2}} \\
& = P(X_i=x_i | E_{ij})P(X_j=x_j | E_{ij}) \\
\end{align*}$$

因此它们条件独立。

于是 $E(X_iX_j|E_{ij})=E(X_i|E_{ij})E(X_j|E_{ij})$

又由于

$$\begin{align*}
E(X_i|E_{ij})
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1}k \cdot \frac{P(X_i=k \cap E_{ij})}{P(E_{ij})} \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1}k \cdot \frac{C_{n-2}^{k-1}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-1}}{\frac{1}{2}} \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1}kC_{n-2}^{k-1}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
& = \frac{n-2}{2}+1 \\
& = \frac{n}{2} \\
\end{align*}$$

由对称性， $E(X_j|E_{ij})=E(X_i|E_{ij})=\frac{n}{2}$

因此， $E(X_iX_j|E_{ij})=\frac{n^2}{4}$

(3)

由重期望公式

$$\begin{align*}
E(X_iX_j) 
& = E(E(X_iX_j|E_{ij})) \\
& = P(E_{ij})E(X_iX_j|E_{ij})+P(\overline{E_{ij}})E(X_iX_j|\overline{E_{ij}}) \\
& = \frac{1}{2}E(X_iX_j|E_{ij})+\frac{1}{2}E(X_iX_j|\overline{E_{ij}}) \\
\end{align*}$$

又因为

$$\begin{align*}
P(X_i=x_i|\overline{E_{ij}})=C_{n-2}^{x_i}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
P(X_j=x_j|\overline{E_{ij}})=C_{n-2}^{x_j}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
\end{align*}$$

并且

$$\begin{align*}
P(X_i=x_i \cap X_j=x_j | \overline{E_{ij}})
& = C_{n-2}^{x_i}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2}C_{n-2}^{x_j}(\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \\
& = P(X_i=x_i|\overline{E_{ij}})P(X_j=x_j|\overline{E_{ij}}) \\
\end{align*}$$

因此符合条件独立。

于是 $E(X_iX_j|\overline{E_{ij}})=E(X_i|\overline{E_{ij}})E(X_j|\overline{{E_{ij}}})$

而

$$\begin{align*}
E(X_i|\overline{E_{ij}})
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1} \frac{P(X_i=k \cap \overline{E_{ij}})}{P(\overline{E_{ij}})} \cdot k \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n-1} C_{n-2}^{k} (\frac{1}{2})^{n-2} \cdot k \\
& = \frac{n-2}{2} \\
\end{align*}$$

由对称性 $E(X_i|\overline{E_{ij}})=E(X_j|\overline{E_{ij}})=\frac{n-2}{2}$

因此 $E(X_iX_j)=\frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{n^2}{4} + \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{(n-1)^2}{4} = \frac{n^2-2n+2}{4}$

## 第三题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/3.png)

(1) 令 $Y=1_{X=1}$

有

$$\begin{align*}
E(X^2)
& = E(E(X^2|Y)) \\
& = P(Y=0)E(X^2|Y=0)+P(Y=1)E(X^2|Y=1) \\
& = P(Y=1)+P(Y=0)E(X^2|Y=0) \\
& = p+(1-p)E(X^2|Y=0) \\
\end{align*}$$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(X^2|Y=0)
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X^2>k|X>1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X>\sqrt{k}|X>1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X>\sqrt{k}-1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X+1>\sqrt{k}) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P((X+1)^2>k) \\
& = E((X+1)^2) \\
\end{align*}$$

注意，最后一步转化使用了第三次作业第一题的结论。

于是 $E(X^2)=p+E((X+1)^2)(1-p)$

即 $E(X^2)=\frac{2-p}{p^2}$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(X^3)
& = E(E(X^3|Y)) \\
& = P(Y=0)E(X^3|Y=0)+P(Y=1)E(X^3|Y=1) \\
& = p+(1-p)E(X^3|Y=0) \\
\end{align*}$$

又

$$\begin{align*}
E(X^3|Y=0)
& = E(X^3|X>1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X^3>k|X>1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X>\sqrt[3]{k}|X>1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P(X>\sqrt[3]{k}-1) \\
& = \sum_{k \geq 0}P((X+1)^3>k) \\
& = E((X+1)^3) \\
\end{align*}$$

于是 $E(X^3)=p+(1-p)E((X+1)^3)$ 

即 $E(X^3)=\frac{p^2-6p+6}{p^3}$

(2)

设 $Z=1_{Y=2}$

则 

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y)
& = E(E(Y|Z)) \\
& = P(Z=0)E(Y|Z=0)+P(Z=1)E(Y|Z=1) \\
& = [p^2+(1-p)^2]E(Y|Z=0)+2p(1-p) \times 2 \\
\end{align*}$$ 

又由于 $E(Y|Z=0)=\frac{p^2}{p^2+(1-p)^2}(2+\frac{1}{1-p})+\frac{(1-p)^2}{p^2+(1-p)^2}(2+\frac{1}{p})$ (几何分布的无记忆性)

得到 $E(Y)=4p(1-p)+p^2(2+\frac{1}{1-p})+(1-p)^2(2+\frac{1}{p})=\frac{1}{p(1-p)}-1$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^2)
& = E(E(Y^2|Z)) \\
& = P(Z=0)E(Y^2|Z=0)+P(Z=1)E(Y^2|Z=1) \\
& = 8p(p-1)+[p^2+(1-p)^2]E(Y^2|Z=0) \\
\end{align*}$$

又由于 $E(Y^2|Z=0)=\frac{p^2}{p^2+(1-p)^2}(\frac{1+p}{(1-p)^2}+\frac{4}{1-p}+4)+\frac{(1-p)^2}{p^2+(1-p)^2}(\frac{2-p}{p^2}+\frac{4}{p}+4)$

得到 $E(Y^2)=\frac{p^2(1+p)}{(1-p)^2}+\frac{(1-p)^2(2-p)}{p^2}+4p^2-4(1-p)^2+8p(p-1)=\frac{2-p}{p^2}+\frac{1+p}{(1-p)^2}-1$

因此 $\mathrm{Var}(Y)=\frac{2-p}{p^2}+\frac{1+p}{(1-p)^2}-1-\frac{(p^2-p+1)^2}{p^2(1-p)^2}$

## 第四题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/4.png)

(1)

$$\begin{align*}
E(E(X|Y_1,Y_2,\ldots,Y_n)) 
& = \sum\limits_{y_1}\ldots \sum\limits_{y_n}P(Y_1=y_1,Y_2=y_2,\ldots,Y_n=y_n)g(y_1,y_2,\ldots,y_n) \\
& = \sum\limits_{y_1}\ldots \sum\limits_{y_n}P(Y_1=y_1,Y_2=y_2,\ldots,Y_n=y_n)\sum\limits_{x_i}x_iP(X=x_i|Y_1=y_1,\ldots,Y_n=y_n) \\
& = \sum\limits_{y_1}\ldots \sum\limits_{y_n}\sum\limits_{x_i} x_iP(X=x_i \cap Y_1=y_1 \cap Y_n=y_n) \\
& = \sum\limits_{x_i} x_iP(X=x_i) \\
& = E(X)
\end{align*}$$

(2) 设 $Y_{ij}$ 表示 $i$ 和 $j$ 认识并且被分到不同组

则 $E(Y_{ij})=P(Y_{ij}=1)=\frac{X_{ij}}{2}$

同时 

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y|\{X_{ij}\})
& = E(\sum\limits_{i < j}Y_{ij}^2) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i < j}E(Y_{ij}) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i < j}\frac{X_{ij}}{2} \\
\end{align*}$$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^2|\{X_{ij}\})
& = E((\sum\limits_{i<j}Y_{ij})^2) \\
& = E(\sum\limits_{i<j}Y_{ij}^2+\sum\limits_{i<j}\sum\limits_{k<l}Y_{ij}Y_{kl}) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i<j}E(Y_{ij}^2)+\sum\limits_{i<j}\sum\limits_{k<l}E(Y_{ij}Y_{kl}) \\
\end{align*}$$

当 $i,j,k,l$ 均不等时 $E(Y_{ij}Y_{kl})=\frac{1}{4}X_{ij}X_{kl}$

当其中有两相等时，即 $i=k$ 或 $j=l$ 时， $E(Y_{ij}Y_{kl})=\frac{1}{4}X_{ij}X_{kl}$

得到 $E(Y^2|\{X_{ij}\})=\frac{1}{2}\sum\limits_{i<j}X_{ij}+\frac{1}{4}\sum\limits_{i<j,k<l,(i,j) \not = (k,l)}X_{ij}X_{kl}$

(3)

由重期望公式

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y)
& = E(E(Y|\{X_{ij}\})) \\
& = E(\frac{1}{2}(\sum\limits_{i<j}X_{ij})) \\
& = \frac{1}{2}E(\sum\limits_{i<j}X_{ij}) \\
& = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{n(n-1)}{2} \\
& = \frac{n(n-1)}{8}
\end{align*}$$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^2)
& = E(Y^2|\{X_{ij}\}) \\
& = \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{1}{2} \times \frac{n(n-1)}{2} + [\frac{n(n-1)}{2}-1] \times \frac{n(n-1)}{2} \times \frac{1}{4} \times \frac{1}{4} \\
& = \frac{n^2(n-1)^2}{64}+\frac{3n(n-1)}{32} \\ 
\end{align*}$$

即 $\mathrm{Var}(Y)=\frac{3n(n-1)}{32}$ 

## 第五题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/5.png)

(1) 由于 $Y=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}X_i$ ，且 $X_i$ 独立同分布，

因此 $E(Y)=\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}E(X_i)=0$ 

(2) 

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^2)
& = E((\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}X_i)^2) \\
& = E(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}X_i^2+2\sum\limits_{i<j}X_iX_j) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}E(X_i^2)+2\sum\limits_{i<j}E(X_iX_j) \\
\end{align*}$$

又由于 $E(X_i^2)=1,E(X_iX_j)=0$

因此 $E(Y^2)=n$

(3)

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^4)
& = E((\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}X_i)^4) \\
& = E(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}X_i^4+4\sum\limits_{i \not = j}X_i^3X_j+6\sum\limits_{i \not = j}X_i^2X_j^2+12\sum\limits_{i \not = j \not = k}X_iX_jX_k+24\sum\limits_{i \not = j \not = k \not = l}X_iX_jX_kX_l) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}E(X_i^4)+4\sum\limits_{i \not = j}E(X_i^3X_j)+6\sum\limits_{i \not = j}E(X_i^2X_j^2)+12\sum\limits_{i \not = j \not = k}E(X_iX_jX_k)+24\sum\limits_{i \not = j \not = k \not = l}E(X_iX_jX_kX_l) \\
\end{align*}$$

又

$$\begin{align*}
E(X_i^4) & =1 \\
E(X_i^3X_j) & =0 \\
E(X_i^2X_j^2) & =1 \\
E(X_i^2X_jX_k) & =0 \\
E(X_iX_jX_kX_l) & =0 \\
\end{align*}$$

因此 $E(Y^4)=3n^2-2n$

(4)

由于 $E(Y^4)=3n^2-2n$

由马尔科夫不等式 $P(Y^4 \geq a \cdot E(Y^4)) \leq \frac{1}{a}$

取 $a=\frac{k^4n^2}{3n^2-2n}$

得到 $P(Y^4 \geq k^4n^2) \leq \frac{3n^2-2n}{k^4n^2}=\frac{3n-2}{k^4n}<\frac{3}{k^4}$

即 $P(|Y| \geq k\sqrt{n}) \leq \frac{3}{k^4}$

## 第六题

![](./Picture%20Assets/第四次作业/6.png)

(1)

$$\begin{align*}
E(X_i)
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} P(X_i=k) \cdot k \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} k \cdot C_{n}^{k} (\frac{1}{m})^k(1-\frac{1}{m})^{n-k} \\
& = \sum\limits_{k=0}^{n} nC_{n-1}^{k-1}(\frac{1}{m})^k(1-\frac{1}{m})^{n-k} \\
& = \frac{n}{m} \\
\end{align*}$$

(2) 设随机变量 $Y_i$ 表示第 $i$ 个桶是否至少有球，其取值为 $0$ 和 $1$ 。

则 $Y=Y_1+Y_2+\ldots +Y_m$

又由于 $E(Y_i)=P(Y_i=1)=1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n$

因此 $E(Y)=m[1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n]$

又由于 

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y^2)
& = E((\sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}Y_i)^2) \\
& = E(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}Y_i^2+2\sum\limits_{i<j}Y_iY_j) \\
& = \sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}E(Y_i^2)+\sum\limits_{i<j}E(Y_iY_j) \\
\end{align*}$$

同时

$$\begin{align*}
E(Y_i^2) & = 1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n  \\
E(Y_iY_j) & = 1-2 \times (1-\frac{1}{m})^n+(1-\frac{2}{m})^n \\
\end{align*}$$

得到 $E(Y^2)=m[1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n]+m(m-1)[1-2(1-\frac{1}{m})^n+(1-\frac{2}{m})^n]$

即 $\mathrm{Var}(Y)=m[1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n]+m(m-1)[1-2(1-\frac{1}{m})^n+(1-\frac{2}{m})^n]-m^2[1-(1-\frac{1}{m})^n]^2$

(3)

当 $i=j$ 时， $\mathrm{Cov}(X_i,X_j)=\mathrm{Var}(X_i)=\frac{n}{m}(1-\frac{1}{m})$

设 $Z=X_1+X_2+\ldots X_n$

则 $E(Z)=n,E(Z^2)=n^2,\mathrm{Var}(Z)=0$

即 $\sum\limits_{i=1}^{m}\mathrm{Var}(X_i)+\sum\limits_{i \not = j}\mathrm{Cov}(X_i,X_j)=0$

由对称性， $n(1-\frac{1}{m})+m(m-1)\mathrm{Cov}(X_i,X_j)=0$

即 $\mathrm{Cov}(X_i,X_j)=-\frac{n}{m^2}$

综上，

$$
\mathrm{Cov}(X_i,X_j)=
\begin{cases}
\frac{n}{m}(1-\frac{1}{m}),i=j \\
-\frac{n}{m^2} \quad i \not = j \\
\end{cases}
$$
